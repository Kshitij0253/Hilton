Authorized Distributor of Honeywell

As an authorized distributor of Honeywell, Hilton MicroSwitch is a well-known company based in Delhi. We are a leading name in the market, offering a wide range of products such as limit switches, PID controllers, micro switches, humidity sensors, pressure transducers, UV sensors, water flow switches, power relays, sequence controllers, smoke detectors, modulating motors, safety limit switches, and motion sensors.

Our products are compact, easy to install, aesthetically pleasing, and safe to use. They are manufactured by our highly skilled and experienced professionals using the best technology to meet high-quality standards.
