Our Company

Established in 1998, Hilton MicroSwitch Co. has grown to become one of India's leading distributors of industrial automation products. As an authorized distributor of Honeywell, we specialize in providing high-quality products and solutions that help businesses improve efficiency, productivity, and safety.

Our comprehensive range of products includes limit switches, PID controllers, micro switches, humidity sensors, pressure transducers, UV sensors, water flow switches, power relays, sequence controllers, smoke detectors, modulating motors, safety limit switches, and motion sensors.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our products are compact, easy to install, aesthetically pleasing, and safe to use, manufactured using the best technology to meet high-quality standards.
